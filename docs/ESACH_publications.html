NEW PERSPECTIVES IN INTERDISCIPLINARY CULTURAL HERITAGE STUDIES

Inspired by the announcement of a European Year of Cultural Heritage, the European Students’ Association for Cultural Heritage (ESACH) was established in 2017 by students at the University of Passau. Today, ESACH has become the first still-growing interdisciplinary and cross-generational network in the field.
